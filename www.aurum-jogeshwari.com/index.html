Overview

DTG Aurum redefines modern living with thoughtfully designed 1, 1.5 & 2 BHK residences crafted for comfort and convenience. Spread across 0.73 acres, DTG Aurum rises with a refined 1B+G+17-storey structure that maximizes space and elevates everyday life. At DTG Aurum, residents enjoy 15+ premium lifestyle amenities, including landscaped gardens, a kids’ play area, a meditation zone, a senior citizen sit-out, and a well-planned jogging track. Every corner of DTG Aurum promotes wellness and vibrant community living. Families connect effortlessly, children find room to explore, and seniors enjoy peaceful, open spaces. Strategically placed in Jogeshwari, DTG Aurum provides excellent access to major schools, hospitals, shopping hubs, and entertainment zones. With its blend of comfort, connectivity, and community warmth, DTG Aurum offers a balanced urban lifestyle designed for those who value thoughtful living. Read More
