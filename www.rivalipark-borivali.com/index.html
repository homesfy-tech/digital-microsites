Overview

CCI Rivali Park in Borivali East by CCI Projects redefines luxury living with meticulously crafted 2 & 3 BHK residences offering panoramic views of Borivali National Park. Spread across 6 acres with six elegant towers rising 35 floors high, CCI Rivali Park blends sophistication, comfort, and community. Residents at CCI Rivali Park enjoy a host of modern amenities, including a swimming pool with a jacuzzi, a state-of-the-art gym, squash and tennis courts, a box cricket arena, and a multipurpose court. For celebrations, CCI Rivali Park features a stylish party room, lawn, and indoor games zone. Every corner of CCI Rivali Park is designed for wellness, recreation, and connection, fostering a vibrant and inclusive lifestyle. With its serene landscaped spaces and premium amenities, CCI Rivali Park offers the perfect balance of urban luxury and tranquil living in the heart of Borivali. Read More

About CCI Projects

A subsidiary of the renowned Cable Corporation of India, CCI Projects (CCIPPL) is dedicated to creating real estate projects with creative design and a social conscience. Our company's motto, "Building Responsibly," embodies our dedication to creating developments that enhance our skyline while promoting safe, sustainable living. Rivali Park in Borivali, one of the most significant mixed-use projects in Mumbai, is CCP's flagship project. The Project, spanning 22 acres, aims to transform this essential Mumbai suburb.
