Overview

Runwal Bliss at Kanjurmarg East redefines urban living with its meticulously designed 1, 1.5, 2, and 2.5 BHK homes that blend comfort and style. Spread across 36 acres, Runwal Bliss rises around a majestic 155-year-old banyan tree, creating a rare balance of nature and modernity. With eight towers, Runwal Bliss offers world-class lifestyle amenities, including a swimming pool with a kids’ pool, a multipurpose hall, a squash court, a tennis court, and a cozy mini theatre. Residents at Runwal Bliss can also enjoy landscaped gardens and vibrant indoor games facilities, making every day refreshing. Runwal Bliss is more than a home—it fosters community, wellness, and togetherness where families bond and children thrive. Designed to elevate living, Runwal Bliss creates an inclusive environment that celebrates comfort, style, and harmony at every step. Read More
