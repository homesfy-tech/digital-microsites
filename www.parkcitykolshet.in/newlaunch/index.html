Welcome to Kalpataru Park City

Kalpataru Park City in Thane is a 100+ acre integrated township offering meticulously designed 2, 3 & 4 BHK residences. Designed by six global experts, including AEDAS, HB Design, and Arup, it features unique phases—Sunrise, Immensa & Launch Code Starlight. With 20+ acres of Namo Grand Central Park, a 3-acre lake zone, 4,500+ trees, and oxygen-rich air, it fosters a vibrant ecosystem. Thoughtfully planned roads ensure smooth traffic flow for 25 years. A 2-acre clubhouse boasts 30+ world-class amenities, including a gym, squash court, music studio, mini-theater, and themed gardens. Over 2 lakh sq. ft. of retail space and multi-level community zones enhance convenience.

About Kalpataru Group

Kalpataru Limited is one of India's top real estate firms, founded in 1969. The company develops and constructs upscale residential towers, gated communities, townships, and office properties using more than five decades of excellence in real estate. Kalpataru is a holder of numerous international honours for design and quality. It has been a pioneer in real estate development for more than a century, establishing distinguishing landmarks and raising the bar.
