Overview

Pristine Kyra Viman Nagar stands as a premium address in Pune, offering seamless connectivity to major business hubs and city hotspots. Pristine Kyra brings you 3, 3.5 and 4 BHK residences that are spacious, bright, and thoughtfully designed for modern living. With world-class amenities—including a gym, swimming pool, billiards zone, children’s play spaces, temple, and round-the-clock security—Pristine Kyra creates an elevated lifestyle experience. Set in a prime neighbourhood close to schools, IT hubs, healthcare, and entertainment, Pristine Kyra ensures unmatched convenience for professionals, families, and students. The gated community fosters a like-minded environment, while ample parking and smooth access to highways and public transport enhance everyday ease.
