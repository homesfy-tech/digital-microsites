Overview

Welcome to Prestige Southern Star


Prestige Southern Star at Bannerghatta Road redefines luxury living with 3 & 4 BHK residences crafted for modern lifestyles. Spanning 32.5 acres, Prestige Southern Star features 14 elegant towers rising up to 27 floors, designed to maximize space, natural light, and ventilation, all Vaastu-compliant. Prestige Southern Star offers a secure, self-contained community where safety is paramount, equipped with advanced fire detection systems, CCTV surveillance, and manned entry points. At Prestige Southern Star, all vehicle traffic goes directly to parking, preserving open spaces above ground for children and seniors to enjoy freely. Thoughtfully designed, Prestige Southern Star blends luxury and convenience to provide an ideal home for your family. Choose Prestige Southern Star for a harmonious lifestyle that balances comfort, security, and modernity. Prestige Southern Star truly sets a new benchmark for sophisticated living on Bannerghatta Road.
