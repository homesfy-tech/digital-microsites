Overview

Godrej The Gale at Godrej Park World, Pune, offers 2- and 3-BHK sky residences that define modern elegance. Godrej The Gale features just 450 exclusive homes across 5 iconic towers rising 36 floors, ensuring privacy and low-density living. Spanning 6.2 acres, Godrej The Gale blends nature, luxury, and thoughtful design seamlessly. At Godrej The Gale, residents enjoy a 21,000 sq. ft. clubhouse and world-class amenities, including a Kids’ Play Area, Multipurpose Fields, Amphitheatre, Camping by a Brook, and Water Plaza. Godrej The Gale fosters an active, vibrant lifestyle while inspiring wellness and joy. The community at Godrej The Gale encourages connections, collaboration, and shared experiences. Families thrive as children play freely, neighbours bond, and weekends become celebrations.
