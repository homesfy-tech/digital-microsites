Overview

Welcome to Ramky Odyssey


Experience elevated living at Ramky Odyssey, a premium residential project by Ramky Estates at Kokapet, Hyderabad. Spread across 5.3 acres, Ramky Odyssey features 3 elegant towers housing 783 thoughtfully designed 3, 3.5 & 4 BHK homes. Residents enjoy access to a 50,000 sq. ft. clubhouse and 45+ world-class amenities. Dive into the pool with a toddler’s zone, or recharge in the air-conditioned gym, yoga, meditation, and aerobics rooms. Ramky Odyssey also includes a crèche, guest rooms, ATM, indoor badminton and squash courts. Enjoy table tennis, billiards, chess, and carroms in the indoor games room. Ramky Odyssey offers a vibrant lifestyle designed for comfort, wellness, and community living.
