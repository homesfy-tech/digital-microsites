Overview

Experience elevated living at Kohinoor Royale Towers, an Art Deco-inspired creation by Kohinoor Group in Hinjewadi, Pune. Spread across 2.58 acres, Kohinoor Royale Towers offers elegant 2 & 3 BHK residences with spacious, well-ventilated layouts and lush green surroundings. Designed for modern lifestyles, Kohinoor Royale Towers blends luxury, comfort, and community living in perfect harmony. Residents can enjoy premium amenities, including a state-of-the-art gym, jogging track, vibrant clubhouse, indoor and outdoor games zones, and beautifully landscaped spaces. Every corner of Kohinoor Royale Towers reflects sophistication and thoughtful design, creating an environment where residents connect, celebrate, and grow together. With its blend of style and serenity, Kohinoor Royale Towers redefines refined urban living—where every day feels extraordinary and every moment feels like home. Read More

In 1979, Kohinoor began its journey into the real estate development industry with a standalone residential construction project in Dadar. Like education, this activity has increased over time, and Kohinoor currently has two massive projects underway. The first is the Kohinoor City development at Kurla-Vidyavihar (W), which was the city of Mumbai's first integrated development project.
