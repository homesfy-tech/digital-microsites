Overview

Discover elevated living at Nivasa Enchante, an exceptional development by Nivasa Group in Dhanori - Lohegaon, Pune. Spanning 4.15 acres, Nivasa Enchante features 9 elegant towers offering over 672 spacious 2 and 3 BHK apartments, each designed to blend comfort with style. The project promises a lifestyle of luxury with 25+ modern amenities, including a clubhouse, yoga and meditation zone, and multipurpose court. Eco-friendly features and an earthquake-resistant structure enhance its appeal. Nivasa Enchante is strategically located near top schools, hospitals, and shopping centers, ensuring convenience at every turn.
