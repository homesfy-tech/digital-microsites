Welcome To Dosti West County

Dosti West County by Dosti Realty is a sprawling 100-acre township featuring elegantly designed 1 BHK apartments in soaring 32-story towers. Residents enjoy a world-class lifestyle with exceptional amenities, including grand clubhouses, an Olympic-sized swimming pool, a fully equipped gym, a serene temple, fine-dining restaurants, a dedicated crèche, and a well-stocked library. Every aspect is crafted to elevate urban living, offering comfort, convenience, and luxury in one vibrant community.

About Dosti Realty

Dosti Realty was founded in 1980, and its core values are dependability, teamwork, and mutual respect. Through time, the company has earned a reputation for aesthetics, creativity, quality, and prompt delivery values that has built enduring relationships. Dosti Realty takes you one step closer to the most fulfilling experience of your life with a vow to innovate consistently and succeed. Dosti Realty has delivered more than 117 properties, giving more than 8,600 families and 42,700 people homes. The company has experience in various development types, including residential, commercial, retail, and IT parks. Its portfolio spans 10 million square feet across Mumbai.
