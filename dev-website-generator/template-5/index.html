Overview

Rustomjee Verdant Vistas at Majiwada, Thane, is a new launch that redefines luxury living with meticulously designed 3 & 4 BHK residences crafted for modern investors and discerning homeowners. Rustomjee Verdant Vistas blends contemporary aesthetics with world-class amenities, including a swimming pool, gym, indoor games room, children’s play area, power backup, and fire-fighting system. Set against the tranquil backdrop of Parsik Hill and Creek, Rustomjee Verdant Vistas offers a refreshing escape for nature and trekking lovers. The iconic Jain Trimandir is just a short walk away, while Thane Station is a quick 10-minute drive away, ensuring effortless connectivity. For leisure and entertainment, Viviana Mall and High Street Mall are within easy reach.
