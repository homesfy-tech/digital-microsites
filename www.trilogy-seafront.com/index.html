Overview

Godrej Trilogy Seafront Worli by Godrej Properties redefines luxury living in the heart of Mumbai’s elite neighbourhood — Worli. Sprawled across 5 acres, Godrej Trilogy Seafront Worli stands tall with 2 iconic 50-storey towers offering 3 & 4 BHK sea-deck residences with panoramic views of the Arabian Sea. Every home at Godrej Worli blends elegance, comfort, and sophistication, crafted for those who seek perfection. Experience world-class indulgence with amenities such as a grand banquet hall, a luxury spa, an indoor games arena, and serene water features. Godrej Trilogy Seafront Worli elevates modern living through thoughtful design and unmatched craftsmanship. Strategically located, Godrej Trilogy Seafront Worli offers seamless connectivity to the Eastern Freeway, Bandra-Worli Sea Link, and Western Express Highway. With Godrej Trilogy Seafront Worli, every sunrise feels like a masterpiece, every evening whispers serenity, and every day promises a life that’s as extraordinary as the view from your home.
