Overview

Piramal Aranya, by Piramal Realty, transforms Byculla living with lavish 2, 3, 4, and 5 BHK homes set across 7 sprawling acres. Piramal Aranya offers breathtaking 360-degree views from Rani Baug’s lush botanical gardens to the vibrant eastern harbour and city skyline. At Piramal Aranya, residents thrive in a lively community enriched by over 40 world-class amenities. Whether you unwind in the cabana, join cultural events at the amphitheatre, or play at the multi-use sports courts and kids’ areas, Piramal Aranya encourages vibrant connections. Wellness seekers find harmony on the jogging track and yoga lawn, while families bond in themed gardens and picnic spots. Piramal Aranya is more than luxury living; it’s a dynamic neighbourhood where friendships grow and memories flourish.
