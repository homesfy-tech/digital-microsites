Overview

Lodha Camelot reimagines luxury living in Kharadi, Pune, with finely crafted 3, 3.5, 4.5 BHK homes, garden residences, and penthouses. Spread across 10 acres, Lodha Camelot blends 80% open greens with elegant, purposefully shaped spaces. Lodha Camelot rises from 11 iconic towers, each with G+24 storeys and just four residences per floor, offering privacy and sophistication throughout. At Lodha Camelot, residents enjoy a lavish 30,000 sq. ft. grand clubhouse and a curated mix of modern lifestyle amenities. The community brings together a padel court, pickleball court, three indoor badminton courts, a 5-a-side football court, and a vibrant indoor games zone—ensuring recreation and well-being flourish every day. With 5-star hospitality by Saint Amand, refined interiors, and global design collaborations, Lodha Camelot transforms daily life into an experience of rare distinction. Here, tranquillity fills every space, and each moment unfolds with the finesse reserved for true connoisseurs. Read More

About Lodha Group

Lodha Group is one of India's most reputed and trusted real estate developers. Since 1980, the company has established itself as a reliable builder in the Indian real estate market. The developer is committed to quality, innovation, and professionalism. Lodha Group has earned many awards and accolades over the years. India Realty Awards has recognized them four times as "Builder of the Year." In 2016, the Economic Times awarded the group the "Most Trusted Real Estate Brand" award, recognizing the group as one of the country's most trusted real estate developers. Lodha Group is known for creating luxurious, high-end commercial and residential spaces. Lodha Bellissimo, Lodha The Park, and Lodha Fiorenza are some of their most prestigious projects in Mumbai.
