Overview

L&T Elixir Reserve is a beautiful, sprawling residential address in the charming locale of Powai. The lavish & largest 2, 3 & 4 BHK flats offer forest & lake scenic views, super comfortable living spaces, a walk-in wardrobe & 2 spacious balconies in each flat. You will be encircled by an enchanting forest, a shimmering lake & a green hillock. With 25+ luxurious experiences awaited, you benefit from a lavish pool, cycling track, juice bar, reflexology path, pet corner, camping site & lot more. For the experience of limitless living at L&T Elixir Reserve, you will have easy east & west connectivity and conveniences at a walkable distance. Seven Hills Hospital, Powai Lake, IIT Bombay, Bombay Scottish School, Galleria MalL&The Renaissance Hotel are within 15 minutes. It is also in easy proximity to JVLR, Sakinaka Metro Line, International Airport & the Eastern as well as Western Express Highways. Overall, L&T Elixir Reserve is a posh ecosystem with an incredible fusion of a luxe-crafted lifestyle and the natural landscapes of Powai. Read More
