Welcome to Wadhwa The Haven

Wadhwa The Haven is an exclusive luxury enclave nestled within the expansive Wadhwa Wise City township in Panvel, offering a truly elevated living experience. Designed for those who seek peace, space, and sophistication, Wadhwa The Haven brings you a curated selection of hillside villas, villa plots, 3 BHK townhouses, and grand 4 BHK bungalows. Each residence at Wadhwa The Haven features spacious layouts, expansive balconies, open verandahs, and private terraces that offer spectacular views of the serene Prabalmachi hills.

Spread across 30 acres of lush landscape, Wadhwa The Haven seamlessly blends architectural excellence with the calm of nature. Residents will enjoy more than 15 lifestyle amenities, including a themed flower garden, jogging track, open-air gym, modern clubhouse, meditation zones, and more. Every corner of Wadhwa The Haven is designed to inspire relaxation and rejuvenation.

As part of Wadhwa Wise City, this gated community is surrounded by over 53,000 acres of protected greenery, ensuring the cleanest air and most scenic surroundings in Navi Mumbai. With the upcoming Mumbai Trans Harbour Sea Link, Wadhwa The Haven offers unmatched connectivity to South Mumbai, making it a rare combination of nature and convenience.

Choose Wadhwa The Haven in Wadhwa Wise City Panvel—where luxury villas meet green living, and the future feels like home.

About Wadhwa Group

Over half a century ago, the Wadhwa Group was founded on trust and belief by customers and stakeholders. One of Mumbai's top real estate developers, the group currently develops residential, commercial, and township projects covering approximately 4.21 million square meters of developed, ongoing, and future (45 million square feet). A combination of timely completion of projects and strong planning and design innovation allows the group to distinguish itself from its competitors. Moreover, the organization has a reputation for providing a professional and employee-friendly environment.
