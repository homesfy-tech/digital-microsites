Overview

Ten BKC by Adani Realty rises in the heart of Bandra East, setting a new benchmark in luxury living. Ten BKC showcases cutting-edge design and flawless execution that surpass global expectations. Spanning 4.98 acres, Ten BKC offers meticulously crafted 3, 4, and 5 BHK residences with Arabian Sea views from upper levels. With 15 elegant towers and 29 storeys, Ten BKC creates a premium urban address with only four apartments per floor. Residents at Ten BKC enjoy modern lifestyle amenities, including a grand 35,000 sq. ft. clubhouse designed for leisure, wellness, and recreation. Thoughtfully tiered landscape gardens, walking paths, a kids’ zone, cabana-lined pools, a reflexology trail, and serene senior spaces enrich everyday living. Ten BKC fosters a vibrant, inclusive community where shared spaces inspire connection, and every moment reflects refined comfort and belonging. Read More
