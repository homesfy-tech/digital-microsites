Overview

Experience refined living at Kasturi Balmoral Riverside, where elegance, comfort, and convenience come together effortlessly. Crafted by the renowned Kasturi Group, Kasturi Balmoral Riverside offers luxurious 3 & 4 BHK residences in Balewadi, blending modern architecture with nature’s tranquillity. Set along the scenic riverside, Kasturi Balmoral Riverside offers premium amenities, including a rooftop infinity pool, a state-of-the-art fitness centre, landscaped gardens, and peaceful leisure zones. Each home at Kasturi Balmoral Riverside is designed for maximum privacy, natural light, and stunning panoramic views. Perfectly positioned near Hinjewadi IT Park, Kasturi Balmoral Riverside offers seamless connectivity to Pune’s top schools, hospitals, and shopping destinations, including Westend Mall. With easy access to the Mumbai–Pune Expressway and the city's central business districts, Kasturi Balmoral Riverside offers a lifestyle where urban sophistication meets riverside serenity. Read More
