Overview

Prestige City 2.0 rises on Sarjapur Road as a thoughtfully planned township designed for modern urban living. Prestige City 2.0 offers finely crafted 2, 3 & 4 BHK homes with premium finishes, contemporary interiors, and smartly optimized layouts. At Prestige City 2.0, residents can choose between Codename Prestige Fernleaf, spread across 3 acres with 2 towers and 350+ units, and Codename Prestige Ethan, set on 6+ acres with 4 towers and 350+ well-planned residences. Prestige City 2.0 enriches daily life with swimming pools, an adults-only pool, a modern gym, fitness facilities, and scenic jogging tracks. Positioned strategically, Prestige City 2.0 connects seamlessly to Whitefield, Electronic City, the Outer Ring Road, schools, hospitals, and shopping hubs. With its strong connectivity and lifestyle appeal, Prestige City 2.0 emerges as a standout choice in Bengaluru. Read More

Highlights Of Prestige City 2.0

  • A 6+ acre township with spacious homes and abundant green spaces.
  • Modern high-rises with smart layouts, airy balconies, and refined design.
  • Premium homes across Fernleaf and Ethan towers for every family size.
  • Prime Sarjapur Road location with seamless access to major IT hubs and essentials.
  • World-class amenities for leisure, fitness, and everyday convenience.
