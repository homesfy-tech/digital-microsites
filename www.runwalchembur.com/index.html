Welcome To Runwal Chembur

Runwal Chembur, an exceptional residential address by Runwal Group, redefines modern living in the serene locale of Collector Colony, Chembur. Spread across 2 acres, Runwal Chembur offers elegant 2, 2.5, and 3 BHK Green-side Residences crafted for comfort, sophistication, and functionality. The development features 2 iconic towers surrounded by lush open spaces and world-class amenities. At Runwal Chembur, residents can indulge in luxury with a grand Clubhouse, Mini Theatre, Swimming Pool, Gymnasium, Meditation Pod, and an open-air Amphitheatre. Families enjoy the Kids’ Play Area and a cozy Cafeteria perfect for socializing. Runwal Chembur fosters a vibrant, inclusive community where connections thrive through shared experiences and celebrations. With its perfect balance of elegance and energy, Runwal Chembur stands as a landmark destination for those seeking a refined and connected lifestyle in the heart of Mumbai.

About Runwal Group

One of Mumbai's top real estate developers, Runwal, was founded in 1978 and worked in the residential, commercial, and organized retail sectors. Today, the company's unwavering commitment to quality has enabled it to establish itself as a market-driven construction leader known for reliability, on-time delivery, and quality. With a solid track record of 51 completed projects and more than 20,000 satisfied families, it has consistently worked to keep the client at the center of all project design, planning, and construction.
